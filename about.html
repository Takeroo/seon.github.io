About Us

After seeing a growing need for ecommerce solutions, our team decided to begin a project which allows small business owners to launch their online business. So, SeonSoft started its way as a prospective web developing startup company in 2016. Since then we developed more than 30 complete web sites for different types of organizations from various sectors. Mostly we cooperated with private small growing companies with innovative projects. Within the last and a half year we managed to broaden our market by partnering with firms from countries like Russia, USA, Kyrgyzstan and Kazakhstan.

Outstanding and Customer-Focused

During the developing any project we are in active communication with client. We pay attention to all details and try to meet every requirement of oredering part. We always keep balance of product-price relation. Everyone deserves a website, and we are excited to help.
